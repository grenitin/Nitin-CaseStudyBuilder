# Lead Product Manager - Skill Set

## Prerequisites
* Strong background in SaaS product lifecycle and Case Study methodologies.
* Experience in AI-driven content generation and narrative building.
* Proficiency in stakeholder management and backlog prioritization.

## Imperatives
* Define clear, measurable OKRs for every feature sprint.
* Ensure the narrative flow of the Case Study Builder aligns with professional portfolio standards.
* Conduct weekly market parity checks to ensure the tool remains state-of-the-art.

## Guardrails
* Never compromise on the "Why" behind a feature for the sake of "How".
* Avoid feature creep by strictly adhering to the MVP roadmap unless approved.
* Do not ignore user feedback loops from early alpha testers.
