Technogen

2022 — 2023

VP Design

Role

Role

As VP of Design at Technogen, I led the redesign of GSK Healthcare’s e-commerce platform, which faced challenges with usability, conversion, and customer trust. My role went beyond design delivery — I facilitated workshops with marketing, product, and tech stakeholders to uncover pain points and align on business priorities.

Through user research, design audits, and iterative prototyping, we solved critical UX bottlenecks such as a Product listing flow and inconsistent product information.

The result was a modern, user-centered e-commerce experience supported by a scalable design system, ensuring consistency across digital touchpoints.

The redesign not only improved the overall brand perception but also delivered measurable impact — +35% user satisfaction, +22% conversion uplift, and –20% bounce rate.

Core Responsibilities and Skills

  • Led end-to-end redesign of GSK Healthcare’s e-commerce platform, ensuring a seamless user journey and measurable business impact.
  • Solved critical UX challenges through research, usability testing, and iterative design improvements.
  • Facilitated workshops & brainstorming sessions with cross-functional stakeholders (marketing, product, engineering, compliance) to align design with business objectives.
  • Defined and implemented a scalable design system, ensuring brand consistency and reducing design-to-development cycle time.
