Behavioral Findings

Cognitive Overload

Managers were forced to navigate 15+ disparate systems for a single shipment, leading to high error rates.

Trust Deficit

Lack of real-time tracking caused stakeholders to rely on manual phone calls for updates.

Legacy Resistance

Users preferred spreadsheets over the existing tool due to its complex and unintuitive interface.
