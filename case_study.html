Bio

Aarav runs a mobile repair shop in Lucknow. He’s tech-savvy with smartphones but struggles with complex web navigation. He primarily searches in Hindi or Hinglish and gets frustrated when results are only in English or irrelevant to his local context. Reliability of information is crucial for his business decisions.

"I need to find the best suppliers and understand government schemes, but it's like finding a needle in a haystack online."

Wants & Needs

  • Reliable info on business suppliers.
  • Access to government schemes in simple language.
  • Educational content for skill development (Hindi).
  • Easy way to compare prices and products online.

Frustrations

  • Difficulty finding localized or vernacular info.
  • Too much irrelevant content when searching.
  • Unsure if online information is trustworthy.
  • Time-consuming to sift through multiple sites.

Key Insights

Navigation Fatigue

Users abandon carts 45% of the time due to a 7-level deep category hierarchy.

Mobile Disconnect

Field operators cannot authorize payments on mobile, causing 2-day delays in dispatch.

Blind Search

SKU-based search fails 60% of the time if the exact hyphenation isn't matched.
